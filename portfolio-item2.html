Quotes

Journal of Favorite Quotes

Quotes is a MERN Stack to record favorite quotes using Node.js, Mongoose, Express, and React.

Sometimes I get discouraged. Everybody does. When I am feeling less than at my best, I turn to the wisdom of others for inspiration. I have written this application to serve as a journal of favorite quotes providing a way for others to be uplifted.
